Protocol Overview

DXN Pot

DXN Pot is a community-driven DXN staking pool that aggregates user stakes into a single position within the DBXen protocol and distributes accrued ETH fees through a transparent, on-chain execution process.

Purpose

DXN Pot is designed for participants who want to earn protocol ETH fees without managing individual staking strategies. By pooling DXN, users gain exposure similar to a large staker while retaining individual ownership and withdrawal rights under DBXen rules.

How It Works

  • Users deposit DXN into the DXN Pot vault.
  • Deposited DXN is staked into the DBXen protocol.
  • ETH fees accumulate within the pot over time.
  • A community-triggered execution distributes accumulated ETH fees.

Fee Distribution

ETH fees generated by DBXen staking are pooled and distributed using a deterministic on-chain execution process.

  • 96.31% of ETH fees are distributed to eligible participants.
  • 3.69% is allocated to a buy-and-burn mechanism for DXN.

Distribution execution is initiated manually by any participant, allowing fees to accumulate and minimizing unnecessary gas costs.

DXN Buy & Burn

ETH allocated to buy-and-burn is held in a dedicated on-chain bucket. Anyone may trigger the buy-and-burn transaction, swapping ETH for DXN and sending purchased DXN to a burn address, permanently removing it from circulation.

Execution Model

DXN Pot does not rely on off-chain operators. All critical actions:

  • Fee distribution execution
  • DXN buy-and-burn execution

are permissionless and callable by the community.

Upgradability & Governance

The protocol is designed to be conservative and transparent. Any future upgrades or parameter adjustments would be communicated clearly and are expected to follow community consensus.

Risk Considerations

  • Smart contract risk inherent to all on-chain protocols.
  • Gas costs associated with execution transactions.
  • DBXen protocol rule changes may affect behavior.

Philosophy

DXN Pot is built with a simple goal:

Earn protocol fees efficiently while strengthening DXN through transparent, community-driven mechanics.
