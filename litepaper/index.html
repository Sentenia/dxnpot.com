DXN POT BURN XEN OVERVIEW
Protocol Overview
DXNPot is a pooled fee strategy designed for transparent distribution and community-driven buy & burn.

What it does

Users pool DXN and participate in fee generation. ETH fees accumulate and are distributed by a public action.

  • Pool + stake flow (DXN)
  • ETH fees accrue over time
  • Community-triggered distribution

Risks & notes

  • Smart contracts are experimental and may evolve.
  • All interactions are public and on-chain.
  • Users pay gas and should understand Ethereum fees.

Distribution model

Fee distribution is initiated by an on-chain call. A portion can be routed to community buy & burn.

Execute Fee Distribution 96.31% to chosen weighted staker
Buy & Burn 3.69% to DXN buy & burn bucket (community callable)

Buy & Burn bucket

ETH set aside for buy & burn is not auto-spent every cycle. It can accumulate, then anyone can trigger a buy & burn to reduce DXN supply.
