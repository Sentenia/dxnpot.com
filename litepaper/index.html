Protocol Overview
Stake DXN to earn fees. Fee Distribution (24h) adds Eligible ETH. Buy & Burn (any time) uses Eligible ETH to burn DXN and mint DXN GOLD.

Risks & notes

Epoch + Tickets Model

An Epoch runs from one Buy & Burn to the next. Tickets only decide how minted GOLD is split — tickets do not create GOLD.

Fee Distribution (24h) +1 caller ticket · +1 staker-class ticket · adds Eligible ETH
Buy & Burn (any time) +1 caller ticket · burns DXN with Eligible ETH · mints GOLD
Donations 0 never mint GOLD (anti-gaming)
