How DXN Pot Works

  1. Stake DXN
    Stake DXN to earn a share of ETH fee distributions.
  2. Fee Distribution (24h)
    Caller and Staker-class each earns 1 ticket. 3.69% to B&B.
  3. Buy & Burn
    Caller earns 1 ticket. Uses Eligible ETH to buy & burn DXN, minting DXN GOLD 1:1.
  4. Claim
    Claim your earned ETH and claimable DXN GOLD.
